WEB ACADEMY

As social actors here in MADEE, we feel that it is our responsibility to take part in the process of reducing the underemployment rate in Morocco. We have launched a program specifically to address this issue

Through Web Academy, we offer free training for school dropouts without diplomas whose ages fall within the range from 18 to 25.
Through this initiative we aspire to introduce this category to the digital world and what it offers professionally and guide them on their path towards pursuing a career in the web world.
The beneficiaries benefit from free training in web development, graphic design and digital marketing The beneficiaries are awarded certificates of the training completion afterwards. We hope that our graduates can get into the job market with the help of the knowledge and skills that they have gained during the training period. The importance of this project resides in the fact that the graduates don’t have to look for employers as they can work as freelancers. Hence, we make people’s lives better and fight a social problem!
